How do I use it?

  1. Enter a nickname (under 4096 characters). If you don't enter one, you'll be named "Anonymous". (we r legion du nut 4 get). In private rooms the character limit is 8192 (8kb).
  2. Optionally, enter a room ID. If you want to join someone else, their room ID will be in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
    • If you enter a room ID that doesn't exist, you will be placed in a private room which can only be joined by people you share the ID with.
  3. Be a BonziBUDDY.

Are there any rules?

Obviously no illegal shit. On top of that, there are a few limitations:

  • All strings are sanitized to get rid of any malicious HTML/JS/CSS. Don't even try it motherfucker.
  • There is a 10000 character limit in public rooms and a 20000 limit in private rooms. Only that upgrades character limit. 10000 chars are free
    REMEMBER: Memory leaks (aw snap) are common for people with low RAM. More RAM results in less memory leaks
    • Yes, the Navy Seals copypasta fits.
  • There is a 4096 character limit on names. Private rooms on 8192.
  • A max of 128 people are allowed in a public lobby. After that, a new public room will be created. A max of 512 people are allowed in private lobbies.
    • Beware low end device users, your device might become a toaster if enough people join in

Remember that people can play YouTube videos whenever they want. Keep your volume down, headphone users. Chat rooms are for the most part unmoderated. If shit starts getting spammy, I might start using my ban hammer.
